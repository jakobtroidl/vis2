A stipple as returned by stipple. Has a position (x, y) coordinate with respect to the dimensions of the underlying DensityFunction2D, and a density âˆˆ [0,1]. A stipple also has a relative position that is independent of the underlying DensityFunction2D and can be used to scale a visualization to a new resolution more easily.
